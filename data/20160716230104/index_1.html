    I am a huge fan of convergence, in fact I remember talking about a universal device back in the day when people still used PDA's. However, this device is not convergence...merely a step in the right direction..but ultimately falling short. To be truly converged you have to be able to pair down the number of devices you rely on to maintain your workflow, the BQ does not eliminate my cell phone, laptop and not even close to my desktop.

    Here is what I want, give me a device that has a high definition screen between 7-10 inches, that I can make both cell and web calls on, that I can comfortably consume digital content on (movies, shows, music, books). Give me a device which does all that and one I can do work with, that isn't sluggish and that is compatible with my peripherals. Do that and I will be more than happy to buy one on the spot.
